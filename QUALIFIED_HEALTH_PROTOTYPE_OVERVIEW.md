# Qualified Health AI Adoption Workflow Prototype

## Purpose

This prototype is a business/product analysis workspace for guiding healthcare clients through responsible AI adoption. It is designed to show how a Qualified Health analyst could structure client discovery, current-state workflow mapping, opportunity identification, readiness assessment, and deployment planning.

## What It Demonstrates

- Stakeholder discovery and problem framing
- Workflow mapping with configurable swimlanes and custom task types
- AI opportunity capture and prioritization
- Readiness scoring across operational, data, technical, stakeholder, and governance dimensions
- Pilot and rollout planning tied to scope, ownership, metrics, and next steps

## Audience

Recruiters and hiring managers reviewing fit for Business Analyst and Product Analyst responsibilities.

## Recommended Review Path

1. Start at Discover to see how stakeholder input is captured.
2. Move to Map Workflow and add a custom task type such as Doctor, Transport, or Delivery Specialist.
3. Add a few workflow nodes and connect them.
4. Add an AI opportunity manually.
5. Score readiness categories and capture recommended actions.
6. Add one deployment phase and review the timeline.

## Positioning

This is intentionally a prototype rather than a production system. The goal is to demonstrate product thinking, healthcare workflow understanding, requirements structuring, responsible AI governance awareness, and practical client implementation planning.
